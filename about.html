About Me

Born and raised in Bulgaria

I've been invested in the creative fields for over 8 years with 2 of them being proactive in the corporate fields, working back to back with large and small companies and businesses. The other 4 being invested in learning and taking part in graphic contests and creative competitions. Croporate wise, I've had and ran my own Online Agency in UK London which was vested in Branding, Web Development and Design and worked for two other companies in the same field.


I don't work for clients but with them.

I don't likec working on things that lead you into a repetative cicle of doing. I love working with people and on projects that have a fluid and progressive working field and process.
