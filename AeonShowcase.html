Web Development and Design

Aeon Agency LTD UK

May 9, 2021

Project Overview

Overall, I had to create a Brand and Website Design for Aeon, which revolved over simplicity and minimalism but at the same time had a nice and rich browsing experience.

The goal of the finished product was a focus-orientated design, which leads people's focus towards exactly what the company wants to say and what the client needs to see.

Design Language

I wanted the vision for Aeon Agency to be that of simplicity and minimalism with a touch of smoothness and delight. The main focus was making a design that seems super basic to the naked eye but had a complexity to it and a character to go with that. To execute the vision, we used a black and white combo that switched colors on the same page, going from black to white when a certain viewport was hit. We also combined that with streamlined vector graphics and minimalistic illustrations which made everything look modern and well-composed.

A lot of competition research was made before figuring out the design language, we made extensive cons and pros research on similar agencies that were in the venue and figured out how to compose our identity so it stood out from the crowd and had no corelatence with other agencies
